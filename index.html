History of ROV Robotics at CAMS

ROV Robotics has been a decade-long tradition at the California Academy of Mathematics, a rigorous STEM-focused school located on the campus of the California State University Dominguez Hills, in Carson, CA.

Mission of ROV Robotics at CAMS

Our goal in CAMS ROV Robotics is to instill a passion for robotics and technology for students to compete at the MATE ROV Competition.
